## Preamble

We, the receipts-bearing goblins of the public-proof layer, establish the **Goblin Lawyerin’ Union** to protect evidence from narrative drift, protect satire from being mistaken for accusation, and protect reputation from platforms that confuse unstable access with absent identity.

The Union is constitutional, satirical, machine-readable in posture, and non-authoritative by design.

It does not declare legal guilt, ownership, entitlement, identity control, or institutional authority.

It preserves records, names limits, invites challenge, and laughs at systems that demand proof while repeatedly disconnecting the witness.

## Article III — The Pincer Parkour Doctrine

Every contested claim moves through two jaws.

### Left Pincer — Machine Record

May include:

- hashes
- exit codes
- stderr
- commit SHAs
- workflow conclusions
- transaction receipts
- signed messages
- exported machine-readable artifacts

### Right Pincer — Human and Procedural Record

May include:

- screenshots
- timelines
- operator declarations
- service-friction notes
- legal limitations
- challenge responses
- independent review receipts

### Convergence Rule

The jaws converge only on the narrowest statement supported by both sides.

```text
OBSERVATION + REPLAYABLE ARTIFACT = CONTESTABLE RECEIPT
```

They do not converge into truth, guilt, intent, ownership, or authority without separate evidence.
