# Receipt Court Fusion Layer V1

## Doctrine

As Above, So Below.

Supreme Court supplies binding doctrine.
Meme Court supplies public memory.
Receipt Court supplies replayable evidence.

A constitutional claim is legitimate only when it can survive all three courts:

1. Supreme Court — law, precedent, jurisdiction, record.
2. Receipt Court — source-citable receipts, audit trail, replay bundle.
3. Meme Court — public comprehension, cultural compression, civic memory.

## Core Rule

No priesthood.
No mob.
Only replayable evidence.

## Three-Court Protocol

| Layer | Court | Input | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Above | Supreme Court | Constitution, statutes, precedent, record | Binding doctrine |
| Bridge | Receipt Court | Votes, reports, hearings, legal authorities, query logs | Tamper-evident receipt chains |
| Below | Meme Court | Screenshots, claims, narratives, public posts | Viral pressure and public memory |
